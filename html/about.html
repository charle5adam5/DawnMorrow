Dawn Morrow, B.A. is a Dance MFA Candidate of 2017. She attends Hollins University for her graduate degree. She has been dancing for over 20 years. She currently teaches primarily Jazz, Modern, and contemporary forms. She grew up in Jacksonville, Florida where she trained and taught at local studios, as well as danced with The Florida Ballet. In 2005, she moved to Los Angeles. Some of her credits include: Nickelodeonâ€™s Victorious, recurring appearances on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, over 20 music videos, films such as Walk Hard and Struck By Lightning, National commercials for brands such as Coca-Cola, Tampax and Kia, and has had the opportunity to work with choreographers such as Anne Fletcher and Michael Rooney.

Her commercial experience in Los Angeles creates a colorful backdrop of movement with the merging of her recent master's studies centering on contemporary dance and "branding" of dancer as "product."

She also has an extensive background in the convention/competition world. Her choreography is unique, thought-provoking, and outside the box while incorporating technical virtuosic forms. Several judges awards, as well as first place trophies have been won with her work.
